Wencheng Zhu

拟就职于天津大学智算学部。2021年6月取得清华大学博士学位，导师是鲁继文教授；2014和2017年取得天津大学学士和硕士学位，导师是胡清华教授和朱鹏飞教授；目前在清华大学水利系做博士后研究，合作导师是张建民院士。

I engage in postdoctoral research at Civil and Hydraulic Engineering， Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Jian-Min Zhang, an academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering. I received my Doctoral degree at the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, in Intelligent Vision Group, under the supervision of Prof. Jiwen Lu. I did my Bachelors and Masters from Tianjin University of Computer Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Qinghua Hu and Pengfei Zhu.

I'm interested in computer vision and machine learning. My research mainly focuses on video analysis and understanding.
