Wencheng Zhu

I am a fourth year Ph.D student in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jiwen Lu and Prof. Jianjiang Feng . I am a fouth year PhD student at the Department of Automation, Tsinghua Univeristy, in Intelligent Vision Group, under the supervision of Professor Jiwen Lu.

I did my Bachelors and Masters from Tianjin University of Computer Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Qinghua Hu and Pengfei Zhu.

I'm interested in computer vision and machine learning. My researches are mainly focused on video summarization, video object segmentation, and image matting.
